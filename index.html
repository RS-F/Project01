Welcome to Hamburg!

The City of Hamburg was founded in the 9th century. It developed into an important trading post in Europe fast.
The Free and Hanseatic City of Hamburg is the second largest city in Germany after the capital Berlin and the 8th largest city in the European Union.

The official name shows the city's history as a member of the Hanseatic League. In the past it was a sovereign city state. Hamburg recovered of many setbacks such as the Great Fire of Hamburg, flood of 1962 and several bombings of WW2.

Hamburg contains contains Germany's largest port which is at the same time the 3rd largest port in Europe.

When visiting Hamburg you got plenty of places of interest to visit, such as fish market, Elbphilharmonie and -of course- St. Pauli's just to mentioned a few.
